# 🚀 StoreFlow

> A modern sales platform powered by WhatsApp.

StoreFlow is a modern web platform designed to help physical retail stores sell online in a simple and organized way.

Instead of completing an online checkout, customers build their cart and send the order directly to WhatsApp, where payment and pickup are confirmed.

The project will include an administrative dashboard for inventory management, products, categories, and future business analytics.

---

## 📖 About

StoreFlow was created to solve a real business problem.

Many small physical stores sell through WhatsApp, but managing products, photos, stock, and customer orders manually quickly becomes difficult.

This project transforms that workflow into a professional web platform, providing customers with a modern shopping experience while keeping the sales process simple for the store.
